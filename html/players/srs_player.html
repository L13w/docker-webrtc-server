  • hls.js — HTML5/MSE for HLS
  • dash.js — HTML5/MSE for MPEG-DASH

Flash is end-of-life. RTMP playback in browsers is no longer supported. Use ffplay or VLC to play RTMP streams.

  • For low-latency (3-5s): use HTTP-FLV with flv.js or HTTP-TS with mpegts.js.
  • For broad compatibility (5-10s): use HLS with hls.js.
  • For ultra-low latency (<1s): use WebRTC with the WebRTC Player.
